Initial release: a tiny, zero-dependency console logger for React apps. Supports environment gating (choose which environments may log, with Vite/Node auto-detection), log-level filtering (`debug`/`info`/`warn`/`error`/`silent`, changeable at runtime), namespaced child loggers, and `isEnabled` checks to skip expensive log-only work.
